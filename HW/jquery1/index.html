Welcome!

What does it take to make one of the best drinks in the WORLD!? Well not much actually. Cafe con Leche really is made up of two very imporant ingredients, espresso/ coffee and milk. An additional ingredient is sugar for some sweetness. Don't fret! This amazing drink is very easy to make and well make you feel nice and warm inside.

Ingredients

  • grinded espresso/ coffee beans
  • milk
  • water
  • sugar

Supplies

  • expersso maker
  • pot*
  • microwave*
  • spoon
  • cup
  • mug
*one or the other. Whichever is available to heat up the milk. This is a link to make Cafe con Leche with a pot.

Instructions

This is a basic breakdown on how you can make Cafe con Leche at home. There are slightly different ways of making Cafe con Leche, but virtually they are the same to these instructions.

Step 1

    Fill the espersso/ coffee pot up with water. Fill it up to the amount of espresso/ coffee you want.

Step 2

    Pour the water into the machine.

Tip*

    *If you want to add sugar, pour the amount of sugar you want at the bottom of the pot. So that when the liquid starts pouring, it will also start mixing with the sugar.

Step 3

    Next, start packing the portafilter basket with the amount of espresso/ coffee you want.

Step 4

    Place the portafilter into the machine and turn on.

Step 5

    While the espersso/ coffee is brewing, place the amount of milk you want into a cup.

Step 6

    Place the cup into the microwave and heat to the tempature you want.

Step 7

    Wait. After about 3-5 minutes, the espresso/ coffee should be ready and your cup of milk as well.

Step 8*

    *If you added sugar or going to add sugar. Stir the pot with sugar and make sure that the sugar has fully disolved.

Step 9

    Have your favorite mug ready and then pour the espersso/ coffee.

Step 10

    Pour the milk afterwards into the mug.

Step 11

    Stir all the contents together.

Step 12

    The best step... Enjoy your hard work!
