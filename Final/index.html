Not Me

Logline: A young boy has to decide to either go along with his only friend or turn him in.
Synopsis: Corey is a highschool outcast that doesn't believe in the status quo. He has one friend, James. James is contantly causing trouble, but he is the one person in the world that understands Corey. Corey thinks everything is going as normal, well normal for them, until James starts taking things to the extreme. James tells Corey of his ultimate plan and Corey is given an ultimatum: James or the school.

Snapshot!

Director, Writer, Producer, Editor, Filmmaker; just a few words that describe Wycliffe Grafton Jr. Starting his filmmaking journey in South Florida, Wycliffe has a deep sense of diverse filmmaking from his education at the University of Miami to studying European filmmaking at the FAMU institute in Prague. Wycliffe has worked in many generes: Comedy, Fantasy, Horror, and Drama. He has worked on short films, webseries, music videos, and commerical projects. Wycliffe always wants to take his filmmaking to the next level and seeks opportunites that will not only challenege him, but also let him grow as a filmmaker and as a professional.
