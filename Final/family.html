"Movies aren't made by one person. It takes practically a village to get a movie from the development stage to the distribution stage. Writers, Producers, Directors, Editors, Actors, and Crew are not roles one person can pull off on their own. Filmmaking is easier and more fun with a team. The diversity of thought pushes a project to its limit and brings the worse but also the best out of people. This is a shoutout to those who've helped me in the past and what they are up to now!"

- Wycliffe Grafton Jr.
